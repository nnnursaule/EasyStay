Sensata city

Complain
Almaty, Nauryzbay district, at the intersection of Sabdenova and Alatau streets

Description

Sensata City has an undoubted advantage for connoisseurs of a comfortable life due to its eco-friendly location, proximity to mountain ranges, silence and an atmosphere of well-being. Clean air, beautiful views and a sense of rural life in the center of a megalopolis - all this will undoubtedly be appreciated by lovers of the perfect balance of urban comfort and the purity of nature.

Technical specifications

  • Monolithic reinforced concrete frame;
  • The facade is made of fiber cement board;
  • Improved sound insulation of the floor and inter-apartment walls (up to 50 dB);
  • Wall insulation
  • Interior partitions – gas block;
  • Autonomous boiler room;
  • The horizontal wiring of the heating pipes provides an independent heating system for each apartment.

Infrastructure of the district

The nearest location of the main thoroughfares of the city and direct access to the complex along Abaya, Tole bi, Rayymbek, Chaliapin (Satpayev) streets. A continuation of Abaya Street is planned in the near future.

Well-developed public transport network

  • Metro – the project plans to build a large station within walking distance from the residential complex at the intersection of Rayymbek and Ashimov streets;
  • Shuttle buses: 118, 128, 44, 137, 27;
  • Schools - No. 174, No. 157, NIS, Almaty International School, No. 176 (9 secondary schools. Completion of the construction phase of two schools with 1,500 and 1,800 places, as well as 7 private schools;)
  • Polyclinics - No. 36, No. 35, No. 15;
  • Hospitals – City Clinical Hospital No. 7, City Clinical Hospital No. 1;
  • Supermarkets – Magnum, Small, Leroy Merlin;
  • Shopping and entertainment center – Aport Mall, Moscow;
  • Parks – First President's Park, Alatau Sanatorium;
  • Markets – Altyn Orda, Sary Arka.

Apartments

  • From 1 to 4 rooms
  • Panoramic windows
  • White box finish
  • Ceiling height — 3 m
